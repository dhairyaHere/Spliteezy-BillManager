MISSION

SPLITEEZY application is developed to fulfill and ease out the daily expenditures and calculations of students. It is perfect on vacations, road trips, a night on the town or in a household where you share the costs. Just define a group of people, add expenses as you go and when you are ready to settle the debts, you can e-mail a report with a list of all expenses and who should pay what to whom.

VISION

To help out and simply debt calculations when going for tripswith friends/families. This helps out with those awkward “debt triangles,” where everyone both owes money to, and is owed money by, everyone else. Splitwise restructures everything to minimise the transactions required without changing how much anyone is owed.
